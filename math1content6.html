MODELING WITH LINEAR FUNCTIONS

Tutorial

Linear models are often used to show the relationship between variables. The three most common linear models are tables, equations, and graphs. They can also be used to find the lines of best fit in a scatter plot.

Additonally, the graphs of linear functions can be transformed too. Similar to shapes, they can be translated, reflected, and stretched.
