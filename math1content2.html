Expressions and EQUATIONS

Tutorial

An algebraic expression is a phrase that contains numbers, variables, and operators. They are often simplified or factored.

An algebraic equation is very similar to an expression, except it has multiple "sides" separated by an equal sign. Equations are "solved" by finding the value of a variable.
