Inequalities and Absolute Value Equations

Tutorial

Inequalities are basically equations, except the equal sign is replaced with a less than or equal to sign. You solve them like you would with an equation, by isolating the variable.

Absolute value equations are the same, except you will always have one positive answer and one negative answer.
